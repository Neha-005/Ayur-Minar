About Ayur Minar

Ayurminar Kottakkal Ayurvedic Medical Center in Dubai blends the centuries-old science of Ayurveda with contemporary care and comfort. Rooted in the authentic practices of Kottakkal Ayurveda, we create a sanctuary of healing that rejuvenates the body, calms the mind, and nurtures the spirit.

Our team of DHA-licensed Ayurvedic doctors and skilled therapists deliver treatments that reflect Kottakkal's traditional healing values, providing care that is personalized, compassionate, and effective.
