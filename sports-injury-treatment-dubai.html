Sports Injury Treatment in Dubai using Ayurveda works by pacifying Vata and improving blood circulation to damaged tissues.

Ayurvedic Therapies for Sports Injury Treatment in Dubai

Abhyanga (Therapeutic Oil Massage)

This deep oil massage improves blood circulation, relaxes injured muscles, and reduces stiffness. Abhyanga is a core therapy used in Sports Injury Treatment in Dubai to relieve pain and support muscle recovery.

Kizhi (Herbal Poultice Therapy)

Warm herbal bundles are gently applied to affected areas to ease swelling, improve mobility, and accelerate healing. This therapy is commonly used for muscle and joint injuries.

Pizhichil (Oil Bath Therapy)

A continuous stream of warm medicated oil is poured over the body to nourish muscles, strengthen joints, and enhance flexibility. It is especially beneficial for athletes recovering from injuries and fatigue.

Lepam (Herbal Paste Application)

Herbal pastes are applied directly to injured areas to reduce inflammation and promote tissue repair. Lepam is particularly effective for acute injuries and localized pain management.

Udvarthanam (Herbal Powder Massage)

This specialized dry massage uses herbal powders to stimulate circulation, reduce muscle stiffness, and improve tissue strength. Udvarthanam supports detoxification and enhances recovery, making it a valuable therapy in holistic Sports Injury Treatment in Dubai..
